About Me

My Name is Ebony Brown. I am a 28 year old full stack developer in training. I live in Nashville Tennessee but born and raised in Rockford Illinois. One of my all time favorite passtime is to try new restaurants for brunch. In my free time I love to date my city and explore the different activies around the city. I am a mother to an awesome little 10 month old boy. He is the reason for my strength and determination

I love technology and hope to be a leader in the tech field for women of color with this Full Stack Developer program.
